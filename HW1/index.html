About Me

Education and Experience

My name is Kaeli Moore and I am a Game Design Major and will be graduating this spring with a BFA in Game Design and Interactive Media as well as a certificate in Web Design. I despite entering this major to pursue art have fallen in love with coding (well as much as anyone can). I have had the fortune to work with Muteki and have gained experience playtesting and picking up as much information from my coworkers as possible. Ideally I will pursue a career in game development post grad but I will have to see what the world has in stor for me.

Q&As

What do you see when you look at the world?

I see a place that is full of beauty despite all that is wrong with it. People who are all just figuring thing sout as they go. Everyone you see is living life for the first time, each day holding something new for us all. There is so much joy to be found as long as you are willing to see it.

What is the most pressing issue that we face today?

I think the most pressing issue we face at the moment is that we are taking multiple steps backwards in terms of equality. People no matter their gender, sexuality, ethnicity or anything should be treated differently or given a different set or rights for being who they want to be. It is absolutely abhorent that we have not all come to this conclusion. People shoul dbe able to live their lives as they please as long as they are not harming those around them.

What do you do to confront it?

I find confort in the fact that no matter what I can only control my actions and how I carry myself. At the end of the day my life is only as good as I make it, and as long as I kind find joy in little things and fulfill my passions I will lead a life I am proud of.

Do you think games can help us solve some of our problems?

I think that games cans solve some problems with their ability to share stories, perspectives and present them in a manner that is low stakes. They allow people to broach topics that are difficult due to people previous experiences. They can also create a common ground for people to bond over which is a powerful tool.
